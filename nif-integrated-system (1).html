Workflow Guide
How the NIF approval processes work
Maker / Employee

The Maker drafts and submits requests for approval. They can create, edit drafts, and resubmit after revision. In Leave, they apply for leave and track their applications.

Checker / Manager

The Checker reviews submissions for accuracy, completeness, and compliance. They can approve, add conditions, or return for revision before forwarding to the Approver.

Approver / Director

The Approver makes final decisions on submissions. Upon approval, documents are considered officially cleared and filed in institutional records.

Leave Management

Submit leave applications with dates, type, and reason. Applications are reviewed by your manager and HR before final approval by the Director.

Memo Approval

Draft internal memos with subject, background, and purpose. Go through a 3-level review: Maker → Checker → Approver for institutional approvals.

Tracking & History

All submissions have complete audit trails. Track status, view history, and receive notifications as your requests move through the workflow.
